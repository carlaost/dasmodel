## Abstract

The increasing complexity of personalised medicine to deliver targeted and highly specific therapeutics to patients for their disease depends on the diagnostic accuracy of our clinical medicine. The very large multicohort study of participants with and without cognitive impairment presented by Ahn et al 1 illustrates the huge potential in the measurement of plasma p-tau217 supporting the diagnosis of Alzheimer’s disease (AD).
