## Abstract

Highlights • Donanemab-treated participants showed increasing clinical benefits over 3 years.• Clinical benefits of donanemab continued after the treatment course was completed.• Amyloid reaccumulation was comparable to the natural amyloid accumulation rate.• No new safety signals were seen versus the established safety profile of donanemab.
