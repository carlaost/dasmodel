## Abstract

Infusion‐related reactions (IRRs) and hypersensitivity are adverse events associated with therapeutic antibodies. Characterization of IRRs can lead to improved risk anticipation and management of this event. This post hoc analysis characterizes IRRs in donanemab‐treated participants with early symptomatic Alzheimer's disease (AD) from several multicenter studies.
