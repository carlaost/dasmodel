## Abstract

Recent advances in single‐cell/nuclei multiome technology have enabled simultaneous profiling of gene expression and chromatin accessibility from the same nuclei, providing opportunities to interrogate the regulatory underpinnings responsible for Alzheimer's disease (AD) relevant transcriptomic and epigenomic features in a cell type‐specific manner.
