## Abstract

Simple Summary The early identification of Alzheimer’s disease can be challenging, which limits prompt diagnosis and management options. In this study, we offer a realistic approach that combines routinely observed clinical symptoms with brain imaging to detect and stage Alzheimer’s disease. The approach increases accuracy and provides practical interpretation by identifying not just important early warning indications but also pertinent brain regions using explainable artificial intelligence approaches. The findings of this study could aid in clinical decision-making and offer a flexible framework for future research to develop more precise, clearer, and more easily accessible Alzheimer’s detection and staging techniques.
