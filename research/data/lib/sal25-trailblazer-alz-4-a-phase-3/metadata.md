## Abstract

The phase 3, open‐label TRAILBLAZER‐ALZ 4 study compared the effect of donanemab versus aducanumab on amyloid plaque (AP) clearance in participants with early symptomatic Alzheimer's disease.

## Discovered sources

**Clinical trial.** The paper is the primary report of **NCT05108922 (TRAILBLAZER-ALZ 4)**, confirmed both in the paper text and on ClinicalTrials.gov. It is a Phase 3, open-label, parallel-group, 2-arm, active-comparator study of donanemab vs aducanumab-avwa for amyloid plaque clearance in early symptomatic Alzheimer's disease. Sponsor: **Eli Lilly and Company**. Status: **COMPLETED** (started 2021-11-16, primary completion 2022-09-09, completed 2023-09-19), 148 participants across 31 US sites, and it has posted results.

**Data.** No open dataset or public accession is released. The Data Availability Statement provides individual participant data (anonymized, excluding pharmacokinetic/genetic data) on **request via Vivli** (www.vivli.org), gated by an independent review committee and a signed data-sharing agreement — i.e., controlled/request access. Additional methodological detail (statistical analyses, run in SAS v9.4) is hosted on ClinicalTrials.gov/study/NCT05108922.

**Code.** No Code Availability statement and no software/code repository (GitHub/GitLab/Zenodo/OSF) found — expected for an industry-sponsored clinical trial.
