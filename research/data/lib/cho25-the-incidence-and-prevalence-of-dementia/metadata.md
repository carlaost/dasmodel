## Abstract

The number of people affected by dementia in Canada is anticipated to increase significantly in coming years. The province of Alberta is expected to have the largest relative increase in the number of people affected by dementia among all provinces in Canada. We determined the prevalence and incidence of diagnosed all‐cause dementia in Alberta, Canada between 2014 and 2022 using population‐based health data.
