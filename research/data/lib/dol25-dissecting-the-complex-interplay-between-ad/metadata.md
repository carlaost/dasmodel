## Abstract

Cerebrovascular disorders are associated with an increased risk for neurodegenerative diseases, including Alzheimer's disease (AD). However, the mechanisms by which cerebrovascular dysfunction contributes to neurodegeneration are poorly understood. We aim to dissect the complex interplay between vascular dysfunction and AD to molecular features in cerebrovascular cells (CVCs) through deep multi‐omic profiling of human brains.
