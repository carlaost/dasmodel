## Abstract

Blood‐based Alzheimer's disease (AD) biomarkers are increasingly used in clinical care, but there are few real‐world studies of their clinical utility. We evaluated the impact of plasma phosphorylated tau at threonine 181 (p‐tau181) on the diagnosis and management of patients with cognitive impairment in a memory clinic.
