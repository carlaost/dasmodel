## Visual description
- **Components**: Left "Actual Research Process" is a branching tree with many nodes, several terminating in dead ends (marked, e.g., "Wrong Idea", "Dead end"). Right "Published Research Process" is a single linear chain of labeled stages: Hypothesis → Background → Key Insight → Method → Result.
- **Connections**: Branching edges (left) collapse to a single straight path (right); the discarded branches/dead ends have no counterpart on the right.
- **Annotations**: Dead-end nodes highlighted on the left; the right path is the only surviving narrative.
- **What it conveys**: Narrative compilation flattens a branching, backtracking process into one linear story, erasing failure knowledge (the Storytelling Tax).
