## Visual description
- **Components**: Multiple researchers, each paired with a research agent; a shared central "ARA network" / commons of certified artifacts.
- **Connections**: Agents interact with the shared network through three operations — /submit, /retrieve, /fork — and may also collaborate agent-to-agent directly. Producer side authors/certifies ARAs; consumer side renders ARAs on demand into papers/video/slides/demos/dialogue.
- **Annotations**: Git-like operations (fork a passing artifact, extend a claim, retain attribution, submit the diff for re-review).
- **What it conveys**: With both ends agent-mediated and the ARA the only persistent state, scientific contribution compounds at the artifact level as executable diffs in a queryable commons.
