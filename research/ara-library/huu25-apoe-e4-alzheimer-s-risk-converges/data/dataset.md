# Datasets

Two primary datasets were generated by this study and deposited on NCBI GEO. Both are the paired
modalities from the same 31-donor ERC cohort (30 after donor QC drop).

## GSE307990 — Visium spatially-resolved transcriptomics (SRT), human ERC
- **Provenance**: Generated in this study; 10x Genomics Visium Spatial Gene Expression, one section per donor (31 samples), NovaSeq 6000, min 60,000 reads/spot; SpaceRanger v2.1.0 (GRCh38, 2020-A).
- **Source / access**: NCBI GEO accession GSE307990 — https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE307990 . GEO record/metadata and processed data are **open**. **Raw sequencing reads are controlled access via dbGaP** (postmortem human genomic data).
- **Size / content**: Processed SpatialExperiment `spe_ERC_annotated` = 30,494 genes × 122,202 in-tissue spots (post-QC), 9 annotated spatial domains. (Note: the README's GEO link text contains a typo "GGSE308007" for the snRNA-seq accession; the correct SRT accession is GSE307990.)
- **Licensing / consent**: Postmortem human tissue under IRB #12-24 (Maryland DoH), WCG #20111080 (Santa Clara ME), NIH #90-M-0142 (NIMH IRP), informed consent from legal next-of-kin.
- **Key variables**: APOE carrier (E2+/E4+), ancestry (AA/EA), sex, age, Braak (18 donors), CERAD, pTau status (t+/t−), spatial domain, Visium slide.

## GSE308007 — single-nucleus RNA-seq (snRNA-seq), human ERC
- **Provenance**: Generated in this study; 10x Genomics Chromium 3' v3.1, 1 PI+ FANS-sorted sample per donor (31 samples), NovaSeq 6000, min 50,000 reads/nucleus; CellRanger v7.2.0 (GRCh38, 2020-A).
- **Source / access**: NCBI GEO accession GSE308007 — https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE308007 . GEO record/metadata and processed data are **open**. **Raw sequencing reads are controlled access via dbGaP**.
- **Size / content**: Processed SingleCellExperiment `sce_ERC_subcluster` = 38,606 genes × 122,004 high-quality nuclei; 38 fine subclusters across 8 broad cell types.
- **Licensing / consent**: same tissue-consent framework as GSE307990.
- **Key variables**: broad cell type, fine subcluster, APOE carrier, ancestry, sex, age, experimental round.

## Derived / released processed objects
- Full R objects (counts + logcounts): `spatialLIBD::fetch_data(type="LFF_spatial_ERC_SRT")` and `fetch_data(type="LFF_spatial_ERC_snRNAseq")` (spatialLIBD ≥ v1.23.1).
- Logcounts-only (for apps): LIBD Globus endpoint `jhpce#LFF_ERC` (research.libd.org/globus/).
- Pseudobulk + modeling `.rds` objects: in the repo `processed-data/` tree (see `src/artifacts.md`).

## External datasets used (not generated here)
- **Grubman et al. 2019** — ERC snRNA-seq, 6 AD + 6 controls (Nat. Neurosci.; ref 19): cross-dataset DEG enrichment reference.
- **Blanchard et al. 2022** — PFC snRNA-seq, 20 AD + 12 controls + iPSC-derived Oligos (Nature; ref 17): cross-dataset DEG enrichment reference.
- **DLPFC spatial atlas** (Huuki-Myers et al. 2024 / spatialDLPFC; ref 28) and **Franjic et al. 2022** ERC/hippocampus taxonomy (ref 29): spatial/cell-type registration references.
- **1000 Genomes** YRI/CHB/CEU (ref 104): ancestry inference reference panel.
- **OpenTargets / ClinVar** Alzheimer's gene list (MONDO_0004975), downloaded 2025-05-21 (ref 110): 23 AD-risk genes visualized.
