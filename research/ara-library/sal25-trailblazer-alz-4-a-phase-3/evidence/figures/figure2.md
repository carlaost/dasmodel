## Trend summary
At 6 months donanemab achieves markedly higher amyloid plaque clearance than aducanumab in both the overall population (37.9% vs 1.6%) and the low–medium tau subpopulation (38.5% vs 3.8%). Both co-primary comparisons favor donanemab and reach statistical significance (overall P<.001; low–med tau P=.008). Error bars shown on both donanemab bars; aducanumab bars near floor.
