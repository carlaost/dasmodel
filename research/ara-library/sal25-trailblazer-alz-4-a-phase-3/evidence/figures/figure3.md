## Trend summary
Donanemab superiority in AP clearance widens over time and is significant at every timepoint in the overall population (12 mo: 70.0% vs 24.6%; 18 mo: 76.8% vs 43.1%; both P<.001) and the low–medium tau subpopulation (12 mo: 76.0% vs 18.5%, P<.001; 18 mo: 72.0% vs 43.5%, P=.022). Panel C: the donanemab KM curve rises earlier and higher; median time to clearance is roughly 209 days shorter (359 vs 568 days), with more clearance events (51/67 vs 25/65). Upper CI bound of the aducanumab median is not estimable ("–").
