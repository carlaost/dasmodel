## Trend summary
The two Kaplan–Meier curves for time to first ARIA-E are similar and not significantly different between arms (P=.438). The aducanumab curve plateaus slightly higher (~0.36) than donanemab (~0.25), consistent with the higher raw ARIA-E incidence in the aducanumab group (34.8% vs 23.9%, Table 3), but the difference is not statistically significant. Per §3.4, approximately 80% of the first observed ARIA-E events occurred by weeks 24 (donanemab) and 34 (aducanumab).
