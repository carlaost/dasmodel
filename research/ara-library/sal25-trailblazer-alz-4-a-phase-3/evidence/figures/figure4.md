## Trend summary
For p-tau217, p-tau181, and GFAP, both arms show reductions from baseline; donanemab shows numerically larger reductions at 18 months, with the treatment difference statistically significant at 6 months for all three (and at 12 months for p-tau217 and GFAP) but NOT significant at 18 months (p-tau217 P=.098; p-tau181 P=.372; GFAP P=.171). NfL rises from baseline in both arms (donanemab +8.9%, aducanumab +14.0%) with no significant treatment difference at any timepoint. Individual LSM points at weeks 24/52 are read against gridlines (≈, digitized_estimate); the endpoint percentages and P values are printed exactly on the figure and in the body text.
