# Dataset — TRAILBLAZER-ALZ 4 Individual Participant Data

- **Name**: Individual participant data — TRAILBLAZER-ALZ 4 (NCT05108922)
- **Identifier**: NCT05108922
- **Repository**: Vivli (Eli Lilly sponsor data-sharing portal, www.vivli.org)
- **Access**: request / controlled (verified via sources.yaml)
- **Provenance**: Data collected during the TRAILBLAZER-ALZ 4 phase 3 trial (Eli Lilly and Company, sponsor). 148 randomized participants across 31 US sites.
- **Size**: 148 randomized (74 donanemab / 74 aducanumab); overall analysis population 140 (71 / 69); low–medium tau subpopulation 55 (27 / 28); safety analysis set = all treated.
- **Variables**: florbetapir PET (amyloid, CL), flortaucipir PET (tau, MUBADA SUVR), demographics, APOE genotype, MMSE, CDR-GS, plasma biomarkers (p-tau217, p-tau181, GFAP, NfL), adverse events / ARIA (MRI + TEAE cluster), C-SSRS.
- **Licensing / consent / ethics**: Written informed consent from all participants; protocol/SAP approved by an independent ethics committee/IRB at each site; conducted per Declaration of Helsinki and ICH-GCP.

## Access terms (Data Availability Statement, verbatim excerpt)
"Lilly provides access to all individual participant data collected during the trial, after anonymization, with the exception of pharmacokinetic or genetic data. Data are available to request 6 months after the indication studied has been approved in the United States and EU and after primary publication acceptance, whichever is later. No expiration date of data requests is currently set once data are made available. Access is provided after a proposal has been approved by an independent review committee identified for this purpose and after receipt of a signed data sharing agreement. Data and documents, including the study protocol, statistical analysis plan, clinical study report, and blank or annotated case report forms, will be provided in a secure data sharing environment. For details on submitting a request, see the instructions provided at www.vivli.org."

## Notes
- No open dataset or public accession is released. PK and genetic data are excluded from sharing.
- Additional methodological detail (statistical analyses, full eligibility/safety assessments) is hosted on ClinicalTrials.gov/study/NCT05108922.
