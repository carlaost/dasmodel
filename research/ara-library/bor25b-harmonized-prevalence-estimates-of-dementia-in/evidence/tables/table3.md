## Notes
- "HCAP-validated" = prevalence estimates generated from the estimation equation using the Hurd et al. approach (the paper's main, novel estimates). "Langa-Weir" = prevalence estimates generated from the Langa-Weir (LW) cutoff summary score (immediate + delayed word recall, serial 7s, backwards counting), included as a comparison scale.
- The Coefficient of variation row reports only Demented-column values: 0.46 (HCAP-validated) and 0.80 (Langa-Weir) — i.e., the LW-based dementia estimates are lower on average but relatively more dispersed across countries than the HCAP-validated estimates.
- Country ordering follows the source table (not alphabetical; not sorted by prevalence).
- This is the paper's stated main result: "Table 3 shows the main result of this study: the estimated prevalence rates of MCI and dementia based on the cognition measures in Wave 9 of SHARE validated by the SHARE-HCAP results" (p.4).
