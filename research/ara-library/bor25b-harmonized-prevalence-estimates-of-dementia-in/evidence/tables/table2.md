## Notes
- "Classified using Manly et al." = classification from the SHARE-HCAP neuropsychological battery via the factor-score threshold rule (§ Classification in the SHARE-HCAP sample). "Predicted using Hurd et al." = prevalence estimates generated from the regression equation relating SHARE-HCAP classification to SHARE-parent cognition items (§ Relation between cognitive status ...; Supplementary Section S2).
- The paper states: "Table 2 shows that the prediction by our regression model replicates the classification results from Step (b) very well" (p.3) and "We believe that Table 2 documents this validity [of the regression-based refinement]" (p.7, Discussion).
