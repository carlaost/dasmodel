# Dataset

## Primary spatial transcriptomics + IHC dataset
- **Provenance**: ROSMAP (Religious Orders Study and Memory and Aging Project), Rush Alzheimer's
  Disease Center, Rush University Medical Center, Chicago, IL.
- **Cohort**: 21 individuals — 13 clinically diagnosed AD with high pathology, 8 controls with no
  cognitive impairment (NCI) and no/minimal pathology. All female, age-matched (mean age ~92 per
  Discussion/Limitations). Clinicopathological information (postmortem interval, age, sex,
  neuropathological Aβ/NFT/other pathology diagnoses) recorded in Supplementary Table 1
  (not included in the provided PDF).
- **Tissue**: ~1 cm³ fresh-frozen right-hemisphere dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC) blocks per
  donor, cryosectioned at 10 µm.
- **Assay**: 10x Genomics Visium Spatial Gene Expression (protocol CG000239); up to 4 sections per
  donor; 78 total ST sections; 258,987 spots total; average 3,505 genes and 9,908 UMIs per spot.
- **Paired modality**: Each ST section paired with two IHC-stained adjacent sections (Aβ/4G8, GFAP,
  IBA1, DAPI), registered to the ST section's H&E image.
- **Size / access**: Raw and normalized ST count matrices and paired IHC image data deposited at
  Synapse, accession **syn53141470**.
- **Consent/ethics**: ROS and MAP separately IRB-approved at Rush University Medical Center; written
  informed consent for autopsy/research use and Anatomic Gift Act signed by all donors.
- **Variables per spot**: Gene expression (UMI counts), spatial coordinates, cortical-layer/region
  cluster label, cell2location-predicted cell-type abundance (8 major / 44 fine types), Aβ/GFAP/
  IBA1 average IHC intensity, derived Aβ stratum (no/low/high) and glia stratum (high/low).

## FFPE plaque-typing validation cohort
- **Provenance**: 9 AD ROSMAP participants (distinct from the 21-donor ST cohort).
- **Assay**: FFPE DLPFC sections, IHC for Aβ (4G8) plus GFAP/IBA1; 722 plaques manually annotated
  (diffuse/compact/dense-core, or excluded as artifact/vessel) by an investigator blinded to HCA
  measurements.
- **Purpose**: Independent replication of the Aβ-intensity-to-plaque-type association found in the
  primary frozen cohort (C05).

## FFPE functional-validation cohort
- **Provenance**: 10 AD individuals (distinct from both the 21-donor ST cohort and the 9-donor
  plaque-typing cohort).
- **Assay**: FFPE DLPFC sections, multiple IHC panels: (a) Aβ/GFAP/IBA1/cleaved-caspase-3/NeuN
  (apoptosis validation, n≈517 ROIs); (b) Aβ/GFAP/IBA1/synaptophysin(SYP)/NeuN (n≈933 objects for
  SYP, ≈507 for NeuN); (c) Aβ/GFAP/IBA1/CD68 (n≈1,136 objects).
- **Purpose**: IHC validation of ST-derived apoptosis (C02), synaptic/neuronal loss, and
  neuroinflammation (C03) findings.

## iPSC-derived multicellular co-culture + scRNA-seq dataset
- **Provenance**: Two iPSC lines — AICS-0090-391 (Coriell Institute) differentiated to
  microglia-like cells (iMGL); BR28 (obtained from Dr. Tracy Young-Pearse) differentiated via
  neural progenitor cells (NPCs) to astrocyte/neuron cultures.
- **Assay**: Co-culture (iMGL + astrocyte/neuron, 1:4 ratio) for 72 h, then treated with 50 µg/mL
  oligomeric Aβ1-42 (or vehicle) for 24 h; scRNA-seq via 10x Genomics 3' v3 chemistry (NovaSeq 6000,
  ~50k reads/cell).
- **Size**: 13,740 cells retained after QC (whole co-culture, both conditions); 3,875 cells in the
  Aβ-treated-iMGL-only subcluster analysis.
- **Purpose**: In vitro causal validation of which cell type(s) recapitulate the ST-derived glial
  response signature (C08, C09).

## External reference datasets used (not generated by this paper)
- Mathys et al. 2019 published ROSMAP DLPFC single-nucleus RNA-seq dataset (44 cell types) — used
  as the cell2location deconvolution reference.
- Mostafavi et al. 2018 bulk RNA-seq co-expression network from 478 aging-brain donors — used for
  ST co-expression module preservation testing.
- Sun et al. 2023 human AD microglial state gene sets (MG0-MG12) — external GSEA reference.
- Chen et al. 2020 PIG/OLIG mouse gene modules; Keren-Shaul et al. 2017 DAM; Habib et al. 2020 DAA
  — external GSEA references.
- Omnipath (v3.2.8) ligand-receptor database — reference for NICHES analysis.

co-expression module gene lists (Supp. Table 4), manual plaque annotations (Supp. Table 5),

10), iMGL subcluster DEGs vs. ST signature (Supp. Table 11), iMGL subcluster DEGs vs. MG states
(Supp. Table 12), and Supplementary Figures 1-10 (referenced throughout but not enumerated as
separate files — likely bundled in Supplementary file 14, a 6.62 MB DOCX). None of these
supplementary files were provided as input to this ARA compilation; this is noted explicitly in
`evidence/README.md` rather than fabricating their contents.
