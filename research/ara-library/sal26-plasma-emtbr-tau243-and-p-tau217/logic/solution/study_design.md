## Design type
- Observational biomarker study using cross-sectional cohort data, with a **primary (discovery)
  cohort** and an **independent validation cohort**.
- Comparison of a blood-based biological staging model against an imaging (PET) reference standard.

## Inputs (index tests)
- Plasma **%p-tau217** (phosphorylation occupancy of tau at Thr-217).
- Plasma **eMTBR-tau243** (extracellular MTBR tau species to residue 243).
- Measured via mass spectrometry (per metadata; exact platform not available from provided input).

## Reference standard
- Amyloid- and tau-PET–based Alzheimer disease **biological staging scheme**. Exact stage
  definitions/thresholds: Not available from provided input (no full text).

## Models compared
1. **Combined plasma model**: %p-tau217 + eMTBR-tau243.
2. **%p-tau217-only model** (single-marker baseline).
- Exact model form (thresholds/regression/classifier): Not available from provided input.

## Analyses (directional; see logic/experiments.md)
1. Concordance of the combined plasma model with PET-based staging (E01).
2. Combined vs %p-tau217-only comparison, overall and at the intermediate tau stage (E02).
3. Association of plasma stage with clinical severity, other biomarker abnormalities, and
   neuropathologic burden (E03).
4. Independent replication in Knight ADRC (E04).
