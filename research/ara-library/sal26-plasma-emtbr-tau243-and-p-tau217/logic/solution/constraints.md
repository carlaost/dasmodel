# Constraints, Assumptions, and Limitations

> Grounding: abstract-only. The abstract does not enumerate limitations; the items below are the
> constraints inferable from the study framing plus the compile-time evidentiary limits. Study-stated
> limitations are "Not available from provided input (no full text)."

## Boundary conditions
- The plasma staging model is designed to approximate a specific **amyloid- and tau-PET–based**
  biological staging scheme; it is bounded by the validity of that PET reference standard.
- Evaluation is bounded to two research cohorts: BioFINDER-2 (872) and Knight ADRC (156). Both are
  memory-clinic / research cohorts, not general population samples.
- The reported advantage of the combined model is specifically located at the **intermediate tau
  stage**; performance detail at other stages beyond "strong concordance" is not available from the
  provided input.

## Assumptions
- A1: PET-based amyloid+tau staging is an adequate reference standard for AD biological stage.
- A2: %p-tau217 and eMTBR-tau243 carry complementary staging information (amyloid/early
  phosphorylation vs tau aggregation). Reconstructed from framing; not quantified in abstract.
- A3: Model behavior in BioFINDER-2 transfers to the independent Knight ADRC cohort.

## Known limitations
- **Full text unavailable at compile time** — no licit OA rendered PDF. Effect sizes, staging
  algorithm, assay platforms, cut-points, demographics, subgroup analyses, and stated limitations are
  Not available from provided input (no full text).
- **No effect sizes in the abstract** — "strong concordance" and "outperformed" are qualitative;
  magnitude, confidence intervals, and statistical tests are not available.
- **Cohort access is controlled** — individual-level data from BioFINDER-2 and Knight ADRC are not
  openly deposited; available to qualified researchers on request (per BioFINDER/ADRC policy and the
  article's Data Sharing Statement in Supplement 2).
- **No code released** — no public analysis-code repository was found; independent reproduction of
  the staging model from code is not currently possible.
- **Interpretive utility claim** — the "Meaning" statement (scalability, treatment selection, trial
  enrichment) is forward-looking and not a directly measured study outcome.
