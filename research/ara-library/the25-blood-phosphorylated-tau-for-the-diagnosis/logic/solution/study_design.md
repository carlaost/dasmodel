# Study Design — PRISMA-DTA Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis

> Grounding: abstract-only. Reconstructed from the abstract's METHODS. Detail not in the abstract is marked "Not available from provided input (no full text)".

## Type
Systematic review and diagnostic test accuracy (DTA) meta-analysis, conducted per PRISMA-DTA guidelines and registered a priori with PROSPERO as **CRD42023422143** (systematic-review protocol registry).

## Objective
Evaluate the diagnostic performance of plasma p-tau biomarkers, and of individual p-tau assays, for identifying biologically defined Alzheimer's disease. Primary outcome: diagnostic performance of plasma p-tau biomarkers for AD.

## Information sources and search
- **Databases**: Embase, MEDLINE, PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science.
- **Date range**: July 1, 1984 – Dec 9, 2024.
- **Target**: studies reporting discriminative accuracy of plasma p-tau biomarkers against amyloid-PET, tau-PET, CSF, and neuropathological reference standards.
- **Exact search strings**: Not available from provided input (no full text).

## Eligibility criteria
- **Included study designs**: cohort, case-control, cross-sectional, randomised controlled studies; adults from any setting.
- **Exclusion**: no p-tau blood biomarker; no appropriate biological reference standard; no diagnostic-accuracy data; participants <18 years; duplicate/overlapping data from another publication.

## Data extraction
- Summary (2×2 diagnostic-accuracy) data independently extracted by eight authors.
- **Extraction/adjudication protocol details**: Not available from provided input (no full text).

## Reference standards
Amyloid-PET, tau-PET, CSF biomarkers, and neuropathology define the target condition ("biologically defined Alzheimer's disease").

## Synthesis
- **Model**: bivariate random-effects meta-analysis.
- **Pooled estimates**: sensitivity, specificity, diagnostic odds ratio, AUROC (with 95% CIs).
- **Stratification**: by individual p-tau assay (p-tau217, p-tau181, p-tau205, p-tau212, p-tau231).
- **Heterogeneity handling, subgroup/sensitivity analyses, per-reference-standard breakdowns**: Not available from provided input (no full text).

## Quality and certainty assessment
- **Risk of bias**: QUADAS-2 (all included studies).
- **Certainty of evidence**: GRADE (per pooled outcome).
