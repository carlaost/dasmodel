## Notes on higher-precision values reported in running text (not in Table 1)

The Results text and abstract report several country ASRs to two decimals, whereas Table 1 rounds
to one decimal. These are the SAME quantities at different precision; captured here verbatim:

- Prevalence ASR highest: Lebanon 828.25 (95% UI 710.40–948.14); Turkey 819.36 (706.20–936.59);
  Tunisia 791.09 (685.56–901.07). Lowest: United Arab Emirates 652.43 (552.16–753.80);
  Saudi Arabia 701.74 (600.97–807.91); Egypt 726.90 (630.54–823.49). (Results §"Country level", p.4)
- Death ASR: reported range 23.36–33.27 per 100,000. **Source-error note (reproduced verbatim):** the
  text states "Afghanistan [33.27 …], Libya [28.04 …], and Qatar [26.54 …] had the *lowest* ASRs.
  Conversely, Sudan [23.36 …], Jordan [23.37 …], and the Syrian Arab Republic [23.83 …] had the
  lowest." Both groups are labelled "lowest," which is internally inconsistent: 33.27/28.04/26.54 are
  the HIGHEST death ASRs and 23.36/23.37/23.83 the lowest. Preserved as printed; do not silently
  correct. (Results §"Country level", p.4)
- DALY ASR: reported range 427.48–577.72. Highest: Afghanistan 577.72 (251.23–1271.17);
  Libya 502.17 (241.77–1123.68); Turkey 491.99 (236.53–1031.11). Lowest: United Arab Emirates
  427.48 (198.64–928.24); Sudan 442.22 (209.16–934.95); Jordan 450.02 (219.99–960.28).
- Prevalence % change (regional) discrepancy: running text (p.4) gives "− 4.9% (95% UI 3.7–6.3)";
  Table 1 gives "− 4.9 (− 6.2, − 3.7)". Upper bound differs (6.3 in text vs 6.2 in table). Both
  reproduced verbatim.
