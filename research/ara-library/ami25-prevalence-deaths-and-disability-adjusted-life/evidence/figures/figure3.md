## Trend summary
- All ratios are ≥ 1.00 → MENA DALY rate is at or above the global rate at every age and both sexes
  (supports C07's "higher than or equal to global").
- For 2021 the maximum ratio is ≈1.2 (males, 70–79), consistent with the text: "The highest
  MENA/Global DALYs ratio in 2021 was 1.2, observed in males aged 70–79."
- At every age band the 2021 (dark) ratio is ≤ the 1990 (light) ratio, i.e. the MENA/global excess
  has shrunk over time — a decreasing trend (supports C07).
- Ratio is generally higher for males across most ages except the 40–59 range (≈parity); for females
  it is elevated in the 40–44 and 65–84 groups (per text).
