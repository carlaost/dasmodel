## Trend summary
- All CSF markers rise across the AD continuum with the largest effect at the dementia stage
  (AD-dem/NDC d ≈ 2.7–3.5). FTD values return toward NDC for p-tau181/231/181&231 (no NDC-vs-FTD
  difference) — supports C02 and C07.
- Plasma p-tau217&231 shows the strongest, most stage-graded separation (d 1.38 → 3.36 → 2.61),
  above plasma p-tau231 and p-tau217; plasma p-tau181 is weak (d 0.26–0.40) — supports C03/C07.
