## Trend summary
- CSF markers are all very strongly inter-correlated (ρ ≈ 0.88–0.98) in both cohorts.
- CSF↔plasma correlations are moderate-to-strong for most markers.
- **Plasma p-tau181&231 is the outlier** (discovery, panel a): near-zero or negative ρ with every
  other marker, including its CSF equivalent CSF p-tau181&231 (ρ = −0.13) — supports C04/C06.
- **Plasma p-tau217&231** correlates strongly with plasma p-tau217 (0.85 discovery / 0.70 validation)
  and plasma p-tau231 (0.88 / 0.83) and with the CSF panel — consistent with it being a genuine,
  well-behaved analyte (supports C03).
