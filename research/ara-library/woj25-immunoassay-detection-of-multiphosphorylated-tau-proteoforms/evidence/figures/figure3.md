## Panel a — Discovery cohort (NDC vs MCI-AD + AD-dem; AD n = 40, NDC n = 15)

| Marker | AUC | 95% CI |
|--------|-----|--------|
| plasma p-tau217&231 | 0.979 | 0.945–1.000 |
| plasma p-tau231 | 0.936 | 0.875–0.997 |
| plasma p-tau217 | 0.913 | 0.837–0.990 |

## Panel b — Validation cohort (NDC vs pre-AD + MCI-AD + AD-dem; AD n = 55, NDC n = 24)

| Marker | AUC | 95% CI |
|--------|-----|--------|
| plasma p-tau217&231 | 0.968 | 0.929–1.000 |
| plasma p-tau231 | 0.910 | 0.846–0.975 |
| plasma p-tau217 | 0.856 | 0.768–0.943 |

## Trend summary
- In both cohorts, plasma **p-tau217&231 has the highest AUC**, above p-tau231, above p-tau217.
- The ordering is consistent across cohorts (support for C03). Text: the p-tau217&231 vs p-tau217
  difference is statistically significant (DeLong, NDC vs MCI-AD and NDC vs AD-dem), but the
  difference vs p-tau231 is not always significant.
