# Constraints — Assumptions, Boundary Conditions, Limitations

## Boundary conditions
- Two proteoforms only: p-tau181&231 (C231D181) and p-tau217&231 (C231D217); no triple- or
  other-site combinations were assayed.
- Two matrices: CSF and plasma. Findings do not transfer between matrices (see p-tau181&231).
- Standard-curve ranges bound measurable concentrations: C231D181 LLOQ 1.25 – ULOQ 80 pg/mL;
  C231D217 LLOQ 0.05 – ULOQ 40 pg/mL.
- Default sample dilution 4-fold in N4PE CSF Sample Diluent for both CSF and plasma.
- All measurements on Quanterix HD-X instruments at the Quanterix Assay Development laboratory.

## Assumptions
- A1: Combining two single-site antibodies preserves their specificity, so signal reflects genuine
  double phosphorylation (supported by the single-site peptide mix returning blank signal).
- A2: A+/T+ CSF profile (NIA-AA) validly defines the AD continuum ground truth.
- A3: In-house Perugia cohorts adequately represent the AD continuum for diagnostic estimation.

## Known limitations (stated by the authors, Discussion)
- **No absolute quantification of calibrator peptides.** Custom synthetic peptides were used as
  calibrators without absolute quantification; reported concentrations are **preliminary, not
  definitive**. Absolutely quantified peptide standards would give more precise values.
- **Potential antibody cross-reactivity** toward non-targeted tau phosphorylation sites should be
  considered.
- **Matrix differences in protein processing** (CSF vs plasma) warrant further study; the C231D217
  (p-tau217&231) assay should ideally be re-developed with dedicated p-tau217 antibodies.
- **Small sample size**, especially the pre-AD group, which could lead to **overestimation of
  diagnostic performance**; findings should be interpreted with caution and validated on larger
  cohorts.
- **Cross-sectional design**: no longitudinal sampling; the dynamics of tau phosphorylation pattern
  along the AD continuum are not directly measured — a longitudinal cohort with repeated plasma
  sampling is needed.
- **Mechanistic claim is interpretive**: matrix-specific processing is proposed to explain the
  CSF/plasma p-tau181&231 divergence but is not directly demonstrated in this study.
- **Spike-recovery is moderate** for both assays (C231D181: 69% low / 87% high; C231D217: 72% low /
  84% high), indicating incomplete analyte recovery.
