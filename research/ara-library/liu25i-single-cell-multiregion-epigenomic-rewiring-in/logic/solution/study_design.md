# Study Design

> Grounding: abstract/metadata only. This file captures only design elements stated in the abstract.

## Design Summary

- **Study type**: Single-cell multiregion epigenomic and transcriptomic study of Alzheimer's disease progression and cognitive resilience.
- **Tissue source**: Postmortem brain samples.
- **Individuals**: AD and control individuals.
- **Modalities**: Single-cell epigenomic profiles and single-cell transcriptomic profiles.
- **Regions**: Multiple brain regions; the abstract reports 6 regions.
- **Outputs**: cCREs, regulatory modules, cell subtypes, large-scale epigenomic compartments, single-cell epigenomic information, AD-associated epigenomic dynamics, and associations with pathology/cognition/resilience.
