## Trend summary
Across panels A/C, VeraBIND Tau positivity jumps sharply from ~4% (Braak 0) to ~83–88% already at the earliest post-zero stage (1–3, or fine-grained 1–2), then stays at or near ceiling (92–100%) through all higher stages — an early step-function pattern. Across panels B/D, pTau217 positivity rises far more gradually and threshold-dependently: even the most sensitive 0.142 pg/mL threshold only reaches 60–67% by stage 1–2/3, and the most specific 0.256 pg/mL threshold does not exceed ~85–96% until stage 4 or above — a later, more graded pattern. This directly visualizes Claims C04 (early-stage sensitivity advantage) and C06 (VeraBIND plateaus early; pTau217 keeps rising into advanced stages).
