## Trend summary
Consistent with the text (Claim C10), the great majority of individual trajectories in every Braak-like facet remain on one side of the Score=1.0 reference line throughout follow-up (either consistently below in Braak 0, or consistently above in Braak 4–6), supporting good test-retest stability. The blue dashed converter trajectories are visually concentrated in the Braak 0 and Braak 4–6 facets and show a clear upward crossing of the 1.0 line, consistent with the text's description of 5 total converters (3 A−T− CU individuals in Braak 0, 1 A+T+ CU individual, 1 A+T+ CI individual). Because no data labels or point markers are printed, all individual y-values are approximate (`≈`) visual reads; exact converter-level details are as reported in the Results text (see Claim C10 sources) rather than digitized here.
