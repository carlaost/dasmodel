**Note.** CU = cognitively unimpaired, CI = cognitively impaired. The − and + symbols denote the negative or positive test result. The Aβ (A) status was determined using lumbar puncture (positive for CSF Aβ42 levels≤544 pg/mL) or Aβ-PET (positive for Centiloid≥20). The A-T- individual with positive VeraBIND Tau score has a clinical diagnosis of apraxia of speech, a clinical phenotype suspected of cortico-basal degeneration.
