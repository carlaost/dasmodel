## Trend summary
- Both panels are ordered top→bottom by descending MD; the reference line at MD = 0 is the p181_IA baseline.
- Panel A: p217_MS, p217_Ratio, p217_ALZpath, and p217_Lumi have 95% CIs excluding 0 (significant AUC advantage over p-tau181); p217_IA, p217_Lilly, and p231_UGOT have CIs crossing 0 (not significant). p231_UGOT shows essentially no difference (MD = 0.00).
- Panel B: only p217_MS and p217_Lumi have CIs excluding 0 for Tau-PET recognition (significant); p217_Lilly, p217_Ratio, and p217_IA CIs cross 0. p-tau181's inability to discriminate Tau-PET status (P-score = 0.090, Table 2) is consistent with its baseline (MD = 0) position.
- Values are read from printed data labels (exact), not digitized off the axis.
