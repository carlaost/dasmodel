## Trend summary
In both panels the donanemab (red) curve tracks a shallower worsening slope than placebo (grey); the horizontal arrow shows the placebo arm needs an additional ~4.9 months (overall) or ~6.9 months (low-medium tau) to reach the CDR-SB change donanemab reaches at the end of the placebo-controlled period. The larger delay in the low-medium tau subpopulation indicates greater time saved when treating at an earlier/lower-tau disease stage. Both time-saved 95% CIs exclude 0.
