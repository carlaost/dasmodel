# Environment

## Nature of this work
This is a narrative review article (Zhou & Glass, *Molecular Neurodegeneration*, 2025, 20:102). It
synthesizes findings from ~162 cited primary studies; it does not present original wet-lab or
computational experiments, and no code, models, or novel datasets were produced by the authors.

## Data availability (as stated by the authors)
"No datasets were generated or analysed during the current study" (paper.pdf p.10, Data
availability statement).

## Reproducibility notes
- There is no analysis pipeline, codebase, or dataset associated with this paper itself to
  reproduce.
- Figure 2A is explicitly labeled as showing unpublished img-ST MERFISH data in human AD brain
  (paper.pdf p.5, Fig. 2 legend, verbatim: «Example image of unpublished img-ST MERFISH (~ 120
  genes panel) data in human AD brain at single molecule level with anti-Aß (4G8) antibody
  staining») — this is original unpublished data from the authors' lab used for illustrative
  comparison of platform resolution, but no acquisition protocol, sample size, or processing
  pipeline is described beyond the figure legend, so it cannot be independently reproduced from
  this paper alone.
- All quantitative findings synthesized in `logic/claims.md` originate from the cited primary
  literature (see `logic/related_work.md` for the specific source paper per claim); reproduction of
  any individual finding requires obtaining the original study's data/code, which is outside the
  scope of this review and this ARA.
- Software/platforms named in the technology overview (MERSCOPE/Vizgen, CosMx/NanoString, 10x
  Genomics Visium/Visium HD/Xenium, Stereo-seq/BGI, GeoMx) are commercial or vendor-associated
  products; the review does not provide version numbers, protocols, or configuration parameters
  for any of them.
