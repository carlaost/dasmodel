# Study Design

> Grounding: abstract-only. This file captures only design elements explicitly stated in the abstract.

## Objective

- Explore the impact of Alzheimer's disease and other dementias on the Asian region during 1990-2021.
- Report epidemiology, disease burden, risk factors, and future trends of ADOD in Asia.

## Data Source

- **Source**: Global Burden of Disease Study 2021.
- **Geography**: Asia.
- **Period**: 1990-2021.
- **Unavailable details**: country inclusion list, data extraction date, uncertainty handling, disease coding, and data-quality filters.

## Outcomes

- Number and age-standardized rates of incidence.
- Number and age-standardized rates of prevalence.
- Number and age-standardized rates of mortality/deaths.
- Number and age-standardized rates of disability-adjusted life-years.
- Risk-factor patterns for ADOD onset or burden.
- Thirty-year future disease-burden trend.

## Historical Trend Analysis

- **Method named**: Joinpoint regression.
- **Trend summary named**: Average annual percent changes.
- **Unavailable details**: software, joinpoint count constraints, model selection criteria, confidence intervals, and exact AAPC values.

## Forecast Analysis

- **Method named**: Auto-regressive integrated moving average prediction model.
- **Forecast horizon**: next 30 years.
- **Unavailable details**: ARIMA order, stationarity testing, train/test protocol, error metrics, forecast intervals, and metric-specific trajectories.

## Risk-Factor Analysis

- **Risk factors named in abstract**: high fasting blood glucose, high body-mass index, smoking.
- **Sex stratification**: females and males are compared for selected risk factors.
- **Unavailable details**: attributable burden values, exposure definitions, counterfactuals, and uncertainty intervals.
