## Trend summary
Elecsys (ECLIA) systematically reads p-tau217 higher than Lumipulse (CLEIA): the Passing–Bablok fit
lies above the identity line with slope > 1 (proportional bias) and positive intercept (systematic
bias). Correlation is strong (rs = 0.88). Bland–Altman shows a mean positive relative difference of
58.7% with wide limits of agreement (−27.2% to 144.6%); text states discrepancies of up to 59% between
assays. Five of 187 samples fell below the LOQ (0.10 pg/mL).
