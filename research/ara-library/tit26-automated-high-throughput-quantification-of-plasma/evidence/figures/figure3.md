## Trend summary
For all three biomarkers, the AD group shows markedly higher concentrations (higher median, higher box)
than the non-AD group, with limited overlap around the threshold lines. Elecsys p-tau217 (B) and
Lumipulse p-tau217 (C) separate the groups more cleanly than Elecsys p-tau181 (A), consistent with the
higher AUCs. Far-out values (red triangles) appear in both groups, most visibly for Elecsys p-tau181.
