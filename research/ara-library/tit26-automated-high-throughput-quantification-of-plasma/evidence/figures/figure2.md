Individual (sensitivity, specificity) points are not printed as data labels on the curve, so no

| Series | AUC (95% CI) |
|--------|--------------|
| Elecsys p-tau217 | 0.939 (0.895–0.969) |
| Lumipulse p-tau217 (Fujirebio) | 0.950 (0.907–0.976) |
| Elecsys p-tau181 | 0.903 (0.852–0.942) |

## Trend summary
All three curves rise steeply toward the top-left corner, indicating strong discrimination
(AUC ≈ 0.90–0.95). The blue (Elecsys p-tau217) and orange (Lumipulse p-tau217) curves track closely
and both lie above the green (Elecsys p-tau181) curve over most of the range, visually consistent
with the ordering Lumipulse p-tau217 ≥ Elecsys p-tau217 > Elecsys p-tau181. The diagonal chance line
is shown for reference.
