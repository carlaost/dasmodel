## Notes
- Midlands, <65: VaD and Other are both printed as 11.443 (identical value). Reproduced verbatim.
- Cross-check against Results prose for the <65 group (used for C05 and its discrepancy note): AD highest = Upstate (80.837), VaD highest = Pee Dee (19.057), Mixed highest = Upstate (3.896), Other highest = Upstate (25.809). This matches the Results paragraph but CONTRADICTS the Discussion sentence claiming AD/VaD/Mixed were highest in Pee Dee for the <65 group (see claims.md C05).
- 75–84 group: the Results text says "The Midlands consistently had the lowest incidence across all ADRD types," but for AD the Low Country (1634.168) is lower than Midlands (1637.489), and for Other the Low Country (224.617) is lower than Midlands (238.800). Discrepancy noted, not corrected.
