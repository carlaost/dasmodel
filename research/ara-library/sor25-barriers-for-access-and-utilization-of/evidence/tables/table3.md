## Notes on transcription
- "PWLD" in row 17's sample breakdown is the paper's own abbreviation (People With Living Dementia / people living with dementia), used inconsistently alongside "people living with dementia" elsewhere in the same table — reproduced verbatim.
- Row 16 ("Cultural and stigma-related, educational") and row 18 ("Educational") use slightly different barrier-label phrasing than the five/six category names used in the Results narrative ("cultural and stigma-related", "information and educational") — reproduced verbatim from Table 3 rather than normalised, since the label wording itself is part of the source.
- Three studies scored below 100% on the MMAT: rows 20 (30%), 25 (30%), 29 (40%) — see `logic/claims.md` C03.
