2. Use biomarker and disease-modifying therapy advances to motivate an updated treatment paradigm.
3. Position anti-amyloid immunotherapy as an early-stage disease-modifying option.
4. Preserve cholinesterase inhibitors and memantine as standard symptomatic therapies for mild, moderate to severe dementia.
5. Integrate non-pharmacological support into personalized multimodal treatment.
6. Identify unresolved implementation challenges and the need for ongoing trials and real-world evidence.
