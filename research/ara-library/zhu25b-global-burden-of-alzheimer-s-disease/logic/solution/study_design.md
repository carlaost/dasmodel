> Grounding: abstract-only. This file captures only design elements explicitly present in the supplied abstract.

## Design

Population-based study using GBD 2021 data to analyze prevalence, impact, and disparities of Alzheimer's disease and other dementias among adults aged 65 years and older worldwide from 1991 to 2021.

## Population

- Adults aged >=65 years.
- Individuals from 21 regions and 204 countries and territories.

- Disability-adjusted life years (DALYs).
- Average annual percentage change (AAPC).
- Fractions of burden metrics attributable to high BMI, high fasting glucose, and smoking.
