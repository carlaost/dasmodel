# Concepts

> Grounding: abstract-only. Concepts are limited to terms used or directly supported by the supplied metadata and abstract.

## Alzheimer's Disease and Other Dementias (ADRD)
- **Notation**: ADRD
- **Definition**: The disease category analyzed by the study, covering Alzheimer's disease and other dementias among adults aged 65 years and older.
- **Boundary conditions**: Restricted in the abstract to adults aged >=65 years worldwide from 1991 to 2021.
- **Related concepts**: Global Burden of Disease 2021; prevalence; deaths; DALYs.

## Global Burden of Disease and Risk Factors Study 2021
- **Notation**: GBD 2021
- **Definition**: The data and analytic framework named by the abstract for estimating dementia prevalence, incidence, deaths, excess mortality, DALYs, AAPC, and risk-factor-attributable fractions.
- **Boundary conditions**: The abstract states individuals aged >=65 years from 21 regions and 204 countries and territories were analyzed.
- **Related concepts**: vital registration systems; health-service encounters; systematic review.
