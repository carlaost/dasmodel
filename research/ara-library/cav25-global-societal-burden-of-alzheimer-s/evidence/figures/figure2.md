## Trend summary
The database stream (AD-specific + broader-dementia search) supplies the overwhelming majority of the final corpus (76 of 81 studies, ~94%); the grey-literature stream contributes a small supplementary set (5 of 81, ~6%: 1 report + 4 SLRs). The single most common exclusion reason at full-text stage was "not quantitative or no discrete severity groupings" (n=367 of 452 database-stream exclusions), directly reflecting the review's core inclusion requirement that cost be reported by discrete AD/dementia severity.
