# Table 1: PICOS criteria for article inclusion in the TLR
- **Source**: Table 1, p.1801
- **Caption**: "PICOS criteria for article inclusion in the TLR"
- **Screenshot**: table1.png
- **Extraction type**: raw_table

| Item | Inclusion criteria | Exclusion criteria |
|---|---|---|
| Population | Adult patients with MCI due to AD | Populations of MCI due to unknown reasons only if mild dementia due to AD is also presented in the study |
| Population (cont.) | Adult patients with AD, only if disease severity definition was provided (i.e., definition corresponds to mild, moderate, severe or moderately-severe to severe) | Patients < 18 years old; Non-human patients; Populations of MCI due to unknown reasons with no mention of mild dementia due to AD; Mixed dementia (i.e., AD and at least one other form of dementia) without subgroup analysis specific to AD; Studies reporting aggregate data with mixed dementia populations, including those comprised of < 80% patients with AD |
| Population — data-gap extension ("To answer data gaps in costs related to care setting and unique societal burdens in AD/dementia") | Patients with dementia of any kind (disease severity must be defined) | Patients < 18 years old; Non-human patients |
| Intervention/comparator | Not applicable | Not applicable |
| Outcome | Direct medical or non-medical costs; Indirect costs; Overall societal costs; Caregiver profiles and hour spent caregiving; Non-medical costs not typically reported by AD cost-of-illness studies (e.g., financial delinquencies, loss in quality-of-life costs, etc.) | Data not extractable (in figures); Quantitative outcomes not reported across discrete disease severities |
| Study design | Cost-of-illness studies; Real-world studies (prospective, retrospective, cross-sectional studies); Systematic reviews and meta-analyses | Pre-clinical studies; Expert opinions; Letters; Pilot studies; Protocols; Clinical trials; Economic evaluations |
| Language | English language only | Articles not in English |
| Geography | None | None |
| Publication type | Manuscripts; Patient advocacy reports | Conference abstracts |
| Timeframe | 2013 to 2024 (SLRs and meta-analyses); Individual studies prior to 2013 included from SLRs if reporting costs by severity | Not applicable |

**Note (verbatim footnote)**: AD Alzheimer's Disease, MCI mild cognitive impairment, PICOS population, intervention, comparison, outcome and study design criteria, SLR systematic literature review, TLR targeted literature review
