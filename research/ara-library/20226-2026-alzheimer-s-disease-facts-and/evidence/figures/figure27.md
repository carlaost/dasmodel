| Preferred source | Percentage |
|------|------|
| My health care provider | 66% |
| Articles or other written materials | 49% |
| Online webinars/videos | 33% |
| Apps on a smartphone or tablet | 29% |
| Podcasts | 19% |
| In-person workshops | 18% |
| My workplace/employee wellness program* | 18% |
| None of the above or not interested in learning more | 8% |
| Other sources | 6% |

## Trend summary
Two-thirds (66%) of adults 40+ prefer to learn about brain health from their health care provider — far ahead of written materials (49%) and digital sources — yet only 14% have actually discussed brain health with their provider (per report text).
