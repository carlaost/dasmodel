| Behavior | Somewhat important | Very important |
|------|------|------|
| Sleeping at least 7 hours per night | 26% | 71% |
| Eating a healthy, balanced diet | 32% | 65% |
| Doing mentally stimulating activities | 32% | 64% |
| Getting physical exercise | 32% | 64% |

## Trend summary
Sleep is rated "very important" for brain health by the largest share (71%); the other three behaviors cluster around 64-65% "very important." (The small "not at all"/"not very important" segments are visible but not individually labeled.)
