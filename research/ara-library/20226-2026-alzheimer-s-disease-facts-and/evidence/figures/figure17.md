## Trend summary
Between 2002 and 2023 (per report text), the share of Alzheimer's deaths occurring in a nursing home fell from 67% to 46% and in a medical facility from 14% to 5%, while deaths at home rose from 15% to 33%. Endpoint values are exact per text; intermediate yearly bars are read approximately from the stacked chart.
