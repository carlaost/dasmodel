## Visual description
- **Components**: Two grouped icon panels.
  - **Non-Modifiable Risk Factors** (top band): Age, Genes, Family history.
  - **Modifiable Risk Factors** (lower band; note "Social determinants of health may impact some or all of these factors*"): Traumatic brain injury, Smoking, Diet, Physical activity, Education, Cognitive/social engagement, Hypertension, Cardiovascular health, Sleep, Sensory loss, Diabetes, Air pollution.
- **What it conveys**: Dementia risk arises from a mix of unchangeable factors (age, genetics, family history) and numerous potentially modifiable factors, several of which are influenced by social determinants of health.
