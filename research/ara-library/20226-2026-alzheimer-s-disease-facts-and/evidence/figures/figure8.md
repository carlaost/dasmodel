## Trend summary
Total number of people 65+ with clinical Alzheimer's dementia is projected to more than double from 6.1 million (2020) to 13.8 million (2060), with the 85+ segment (top band) driving much of the growth. Per-age breakdown labels are not individually printed; only the total data labels are shown.
