# Table 2 - Signs of Alzheimer's dementia compared with typical age-related changes
- **Source**: Table 2, §2.1.2 (Overview), p. 6 in 2026 Alzheimer's Disease Facts and Figures (Alzheimer's Association Report), Alzheimer's & Dementia 2026;22:e71345
- **Caption**: "Signs of Alzheimer's dementia compared with typical age-related changes*." (*For more information visit alz.org)
- **Screenshot**: table2.png
- **Extraction type**: raw_table

| Signs of Alzheimer's dementia | Typical age-related changes |
|------|------|
| Memory loss that disrupts daily life (forgetting recently learned info; repeated questions; increasing reliance on memory aids) | Sometimes forgetting names or appointments, but remembering them later |
| Challenges in planning or solving problems (trouble following a recipe, tracking bills; difficulty concentrating) | Making occasional errors managing finances or household bills |
| Difficulty completing familiar tasks (driving to familiar location, organizing a list, game rules) | Occasionally needing help to use microwave settings or record a TV show |
| Confusion with time or place (lose track of dates/seasons; forget where they are/how they got there) | Getting confused about the day of the week but figuring it out later |
| Trouble understanding visual images and spatial relationships (judging distance, color, contrast) | Vision changes related to cataracts |
| New problems with words in speaking or writing (trouble following/joining conversation; wrong names) | Sometimes having trouble finding the right word |
| Misplacing things and losing the ability to retrace steps (put things in unusual places; accuse others of stealing) | Misplacing things from time to time and retracing steps to find them |
| Decreased or poor judgment (poor money judgment; less attention to grooming) | Making a bad decision or mistake once in a while |
| Withdrawal from work or social activities | Sometimes feeling uninterested in family and social obligations |
| Changes in mood, personality and behavior (confused, suspicious, depressed, fearful, anxious) | Developing specific ways of doing things and becoming irritable when routine is disrupted |
