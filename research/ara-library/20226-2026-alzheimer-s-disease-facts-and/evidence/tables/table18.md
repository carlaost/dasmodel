# Table 18 - Average annual per-person payments by type of service, Medicare beneficiaries 65+, with/without dementia (2025 dollars)
- **Source**: Table 18, §7.2.2, p. 57 in 2026 Alzheimer's Disease Facts and Figures (Alzheimer's Association Report), Alzheimer's & Dementia 2026;22:e71345
- **Caption**: "Average annual per-person payments by type of service for health care and long-term care services, Medicare beneficiaries age 65 and older, with and without Alzheimer's or other dementias, in 2025 dollars." Created from unpublished data from the Medicare Current Beneficiary Survey for 2018 (ref 906).

- **Extraction type**: raw_table

| Type of service | With Alzheimer's/other dementias | Without Alzheimer's/other dementias |
|------|------|------|
| Inpatient hospital | \$8,340 | \$3,121 |
| Medical provider* | 6,182 | 3,990 |
| Skilled nursing facility | 4,261 | 429 |
| Nursing home | 15,715 | 608 |
| Hospice | 2,466 | 144 |
| Home health care | 1,973 | 291 |
| Prescription medications† | 5,119 | 3,452 |
| Dental | 571 | 823 |
| Outpatient events | 3,047 | 2,397 |

*"Medical provider" includes physician, other provider and laboratory services, and medical equipment and supplies. †Prescription medication payments available only for people living in the community.
