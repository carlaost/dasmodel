Each cell contains **two comma-separated hazard ratios** for the two subpopulations that make up the disparity pair. Footnote (verbatim): "Disparities are denoted: Black–White (BW), White–Native American (WN), Hispanic–White (HW), White–Asian (WA), female–male (FM), and stroke-belt states versus states without common border with the stroke-belt states (SB)."
