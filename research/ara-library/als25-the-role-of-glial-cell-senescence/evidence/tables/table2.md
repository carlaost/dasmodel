# Table 2 - Markers of pathology-induced senescence

**Source**: Table 2, page 10, in "The Role of Glial Cell Senescence in Alzheimer's Disease" (Alshaebi et al. 2025, J Neurochem)
**Caption**: "Markers of pathology-induced senescence."
**Screenshot**: table2.png
**Extraction type**: raw_table

Rows = marker category; columns = glial cell type. Arrows copied verbatim (↑ = increased, ↓ = decreased). Note this table has **three** cell-type columns (Astrocytes, Microglia, OPCs) — no separate Oligodendrocytes column, consistent with §1.2.3's statement that direct senescence in mature oligodendrocytes has not yet been reported in AD. "Morphology — Dystrophic appearance (Streit et al. 2009)" appears centered across the columns in the source (placed here in the Microglia column).

| Category | Astrocytes | Microglia | OPCs |
|---|---|---|---|
| Morphology | | Dystrophic appearance (Streit et al. 2009) | |
| Metabolism | ↑ SA-β-gal (Bhat et al. 2012; Ungerleider et al. 2022); ↓ Aβ endocytosis (Iram et al. 2016) | ↑ SA-β-gal (Hu et al. 2021; Fancy et al. 2024); ↓ phagocytosis (Fancy et al. 2024; Karabag et al. 2023; Hellwig et al. 2015) | ↑ SA-β-gal (Zhang et al. 2019; Xie et al. 2021) |
| Cell-cycle arrest | ↑ p16INK4A (Bhat et al. 2012; Bussian et al. 2018; Gaikwad et al. 2021; Ungerleider et al. 2022); ↑ p21WAF1/CIP1 (Ungerleider et al. 2022); ↑ p53 (Ungerleider et al. 2022); ↓ Ki67 (Ungerleider et al. 2022) | ↑ p16INK4A (Bussian et al. 2018; Yao et al. 2024; Fancy et al. 2024; Karabag et al. 2023); ↑ p21WAF1/CIP1 (Fancy et al. 2024; Karabag et al. 2023) | ↑ p16INK4A (Zhang et al. 2019); ↑ p21WAF1/CIP1 (Zhang et al. 2019; Xie et al. 2021) |
