**Caption**: "Markers of age-related senescence."
**Screenshot**: table1.png
**Extraction type**: raw_table

Rows = marker category; columns = glial cell type. Arrows copied verbatim (↑ = increased, ↓ = decreased). Citations retained as in the source. A blank cell means no marker was listed in that category/cell-type combination. "Morphology — Dystrophic appearance (Streit et al. 2004)" spans the cell-type columns as a single entry in the source (rendered here in the Microglia column, where the dystrophic-appearance literature is discussed in §1.2.2; source places it centered across columns).
