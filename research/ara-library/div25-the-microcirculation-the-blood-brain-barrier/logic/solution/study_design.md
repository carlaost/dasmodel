## Pericyte-Centered Synthesis

- **Barrier function**: Pericytes maintain blood-brain barrier integrity.
- **Flow function**: Pericytes control capillary blood flow and capillary stalling.
- **Coupling function**: Pericytes participate in neurovascular coupling.
- **Drainage/clearance function**: Pericytes are linked to intramural periarterial drainage, glymphatic drainage, and consequent amyloid/tau clearance.
- **Signaling function**: Deficient endothelial cell-pericyte PDGBB-PDGFRbeta signaling is stated to cause pericyte dysfunction.
- **Translational implication**: Pericytes are presented as therapeutic and regenerative-therapy targets.
