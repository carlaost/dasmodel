## Visual description
- **Components**: A concentric radial "sunburst" map of the entire pipeline. Three rings encode
  phase: inner ring = Phase 3 agents, middle ring = Phase 2 agents, outer ring = Phase 1 therapies.
  Each agent is plotted as an icon with a name label.
- **Connections / layout**: The disk is divided into four angular quadrants by therapeutic purpose:
  "Disease-Targeted Biologic" (green sector, top), "Disease-Targeted Small Molecule" (purple sector,
  right/bottom-right), "Neuropsychiatric Symptom Tx" (blue sector, bottom-left), and "Cognition
  Enhancer" (orange sector, left). An agent's radial ring gives its phase and its angular sector
  gives its therapeutic-purpose class.
- **Annotations**:
  - Top legend "Targeted Pathophysiological Process" maps 18 icon colors to CADRO classes: Amyloid,
    Tau, APOE/Lipids and Lipoprotein Receptors, Neurotransmitter Receptors, Neurogenesis,
    Inflammation, Oxidative Stress, Cell Death, Proteostasis/Proteinopathies, Metabolism and
    Bioenergetics, Vasculature, Growth Factors and Hormones, Synaptic Plasticity/Neuroprotection,
    Gut–Brain Axis, Circadian Rhythm, Epigenetic Regulators, Multi-Target, Undisclosed.
  - Bottom legend "Trial Population" maps icon shape to continuum stage: Healthy Volunteers
    (triangle), Preclinical AD (inverted triangle), MCI (Prodromal) and MCI–Mild AD Dementia
    (circle), Mild–Moderate AD Dementia (square), Severe AD Dementia (pentagon).
- **What it conveys**: A single-glance map of the whole 2026 pipeline — every agent simultaneously
  positioned by phase (ring), therapeutic purpose (sector), CADRO target (color), and trial
  population (shape). It visually demonstrates the pipeline's breadth and the DTT-dominant,
  amyloid/neurotransmitter-heavy composition quantified in Figures 2–4 and Tables 1–3.
