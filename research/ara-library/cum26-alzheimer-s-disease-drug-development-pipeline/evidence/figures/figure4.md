## Trend summary
Among the 84 Phase 2 agents, DTTs dominate (small molecules 45% + biologics 27% = 72%, matching the
61/72% DTT count in §3.3), with small molecules the single largest group. By CADRO, inflammation/
immune and transmitter receptors are tied as the leading Phase 2 targets (23% each), followed by
amyloid and tau (10% each). Phase 2 shows the broadest CADRO spread of any phase (16 categories).
