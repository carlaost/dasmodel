## Trend summary
Among the 36 Phase 3 agents, disease-targeting therapies (biologics 31% + small molecules 28% = 59%,
≈ the 21/58% DTT count stated in §3.2) dominate over cognition enhancers (25%) and neuropsychiatric
agents (17%). By CADRO, transmitter receptors (39%) and amyloid (28%) are the leading Phase 3
targets. Note: the printed pie labels for inflammation/immune and proteostasis read 6% each, whereas
§3.2 text states "two (5%) each" — a rounding difference on the same underlying two-agent counts.
