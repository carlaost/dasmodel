Plasma beta-Amyloid and Cognitive Decline. Abstract: whether plasma Abeta predicts cognitive change in aging/AD; 880 participants, Abeta40/42 at baseline and ~4.5y. Findings: higher baseline Abeta40/42 predicted faster global decline; stable/decreasing Abeta42 declined faster than increasing; Abeta42 predicted decline across domains; effect persists in cognitively healthy. Contribution: links plasma Abeta profiles to specific cognitive-domain change (not just diagnosis); relevance beyond dementia; plasma Abeta relevant to cognition independent of dementia.
