Association of Distinct Variants in SORL1 With imaging/neuropathology. Abstract: whether SORL1 variants link to MRI atrophy/vascular measures; 30 SNPs, 226 families (44 AA, 182 white) MIRAGE, plus 69 autopsy-confirmed AD. Findings: white matter disease assoc SNPs6-10; cerebral/hippocampal atrophy assoc SNPs21-26; CGC haplotype SNPs8-10 reduced WM changes (clinical+autopsy); low-WM-burden families revealed new SORL1-AD association. Contribution: SORL1 variants also associate with neurodegenerative/cerebrovascular imaging+neuropath measures; multiple functional regions in SORL1.
