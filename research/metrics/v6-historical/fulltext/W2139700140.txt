The Rate of Cognitive Decline in Parkinson Disease. Abstract: measured cognitive decline (MMSE) in PD over 8 years vs AD and controls. Findings: PD mean annual MMSE decline 1.1; PD-with-dementia 2.3 (similar to AD 2.6), PDD more rapid initial decline than AD; 43% non-demented PD declined like controls; old age, hallucinations, severe motor symptoms predicted faster decline. Contribution: largest prospective longitudinal study of PD cognitive decline; dementia risk in PD follows heterogeneous trajectories; PDD decline matches AD.
