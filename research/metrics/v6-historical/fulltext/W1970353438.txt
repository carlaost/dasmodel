Hippocampal Volumes, Proton MR Spectroscopy across MCI subtypes. Abstract: whether MCI subtypes show distinct imaging; 91 single-domain amnestic, 32 multi-domain amnestic, 20 nonamnestic MCI, 100 controls; MRI + spectroscopy. Findings: single-domain amnestic MCI = small hippocampi + elevated mI/Cr (AD-like); nonamnestic MCI = normal hippocampi/metabolites but more cortical infarcts (15% vs 7%); multi-domain intermediate (mixed pathology). Contribution: complementary imaging markers distinguish MCI subtypes reflecting different underlying pathologies; clinical implications for disease-specific treatment.
