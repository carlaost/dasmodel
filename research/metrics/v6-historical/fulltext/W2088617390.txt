The Natural History of Alzheimer Disease (familial AD). Abstract: 19 FAD patients (8 presymptomatic), followed 1-10y; timing of onset, MMSE decline, neuropsych deficits. Findings: memory and general intelligence impaired earliest (sometimes presymptomatic); MMSE insensitive early (50% above cutoff ~4y post-onset); spelling most resilient; myoclonus early; median survival ~8y like sporadic AD; long prodrome with subtle deficits. Contribution: first detailed longitudinal characterization of cognitive decline across presymptomatic-symptomatic spectrum in familial AD; temporal hierarchy of domain involvement.
