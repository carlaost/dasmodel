Abstract: Investigates associations between genetic variants in the SORL1 gene and late-onset AD in an urban multiethnic community cohort (northern Manhattan). Findings: SNP12 associated with AD in African-Americans and Caribbean Hispanics; SNP26 in African-Americans; SNP20 in non-Hispanic Whites; TTC haplotype (SNPs23-25) associated in Whites (p=0.035) replicating prior findings; CCA haplotype (SNPs24-26) in African-Americans (p=0.0006). Contribution: confirms SORL1-AD association and demonstrates extensive allelic heterogeneity across ethnic populations; broad regions of SORL1 need scrutiny for functional variants.
