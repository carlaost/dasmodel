Improved mitochondrial function in brain aging and AD — piracetam. Abstract: proposes piracetam improves cognition via enhanced mitochondrial membrane fluidity -> better ATP, reduced apoptosis. Findings: enhances mito membrane potential/ATP in aged/disease models; protective vs H2O2/nitrosative/Abeta stress; reduced soluble Abeta ~25% in APP mice; improved LTP/spatial memory. Contribution: establishes piracetam as a mitochondrial stabilizer via membrane fluidity (not radical scavenging); unifies diverse therapeutic effects under mitochondrial-dysfunction hypothesis.
