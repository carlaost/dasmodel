Association of a Functional Polymorphism in CETP with dementia. Abstract: whether CETP codon-405 polymorphism associates with memory decline/dementia; 523 adults 70+, annual cognition 1994-2009. Findings: valine homozygotes slower memory decline (51% relative), lower dementia risk (HR 0.28) and AD risk (HR 0.31); episodic-memory specific. Contribution: first prospective incidence study of CETP V405 and dementia risk; prospective designs capture longevity-gene effects better than case-control.
