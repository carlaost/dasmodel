# Prior-art landscape at this paper’s publication date

These are the closest PRECEDING works (the state of the art this paper builds on or competes with). Judge how much the paper advances BEYOND this. No scores are given — form your own assessment.

- Sevigny et al. 2016 — seminal Aducanumab study reporting dose-dependent removal of amyloid plaques in mice and patients [ref 31]. Direct precedent for this paper's dose-dependent amyloid-lowering result.
- Prior chronic / long-term anti-Aβ treatment studies in amyloid mouse models reporting increased microglial clustering and variable DAM modulation around plaques [refs 29, 30, 33, 34, 40]. This paper situates its microglial-phenotype findings against this inconsistent prior literature.
- Xia et al. 2022 — characterization of the APP-SAA knock-in mouse model and its MX-04+ microglia / lipid (GM3, cholesteryl ester) changes [ref 47]. The animal model and the baseline lipid/microglial-state comparisons are adopted from here.
- Chen et al. 2021 — reference microglial transcriptional state definitions (DAM, IFN, MHC, proliferation, homeostatic) used to interpret the bulk RNA-seq signatures [ref 94].
