# Related work / precedence

TRAILBLAZER-ALZ 2 sits in the anti-amyloid monoclonal-antibody lineage for early Alzheimer disease.
Its immediate comparators (baselines quantified in gro/delta_ledger.yaml + external_quantities.yaml)
are source-verified from their own abstracts (saved under evidence/).

## Donanemab phase 2 - TRAILBLAZER-ALZ (Mintun et al., NEJM 2021; 10.1056/nejmoa2100708)
Same drug, same iADRS primary outcome, same 76-week window, tau-PET-selected population, but small
(n=257: 131 donanemab / 126 placebo). Result: iADRS change -6.86 (donanemab) vs -10.06 (placebo),
"difference, 3.20 (95% confidence interval, 0.12 to 6.27; P = 0.04)"; "results for most secondary
outcomes showed no substantial difference." TRAILBLAZER-ALZ 2 is the confirmatory, ~7x larger phase
3 that converts this borderline (P=.04, wide CI, mixed secondaries) signal into a robust result
(P<.001, narrow CI, 23/24 gated outcomes significant). This is the paper's most direct precedent.

## Lecanemab - CLARITY-AD (van Dyck et al., NEJM 2023; 10.1056/nejmoa2212948)
Contemporaneous anti-amyloid phase 3 (n=1795) with CDR-SB as the primary outcome. Result: adjusted
LSM CDR-SB change 1.21 (lecanemab) vs 1.66 (placebo), "difference, -0.45; 95% CI, -0.67 to -0.23."
Donanemab's CDR-SB treatment difference (-0.67 low/medium tau; -0.70 combined) is numerically larger
in absolute terms. NOTE: this is a cross-trial comparison, not a head-to-head - different dosing,
tau selection, and completion windows - so it is recorded as a quantified-but-cautious delta.

## Aducanumab - EMERGE/ENGAGE (Budd Haeberlein et al., 2022)
The first FDA-approved (accelerated, controversial) anti-amyloid antibody; discordant phase 3
results (EMERGE positive, ENGAGE negative) motivated the field's demand for a clean, fully powered
positive trial - the gap TRAILBLAZER-ALZ 2 fills. Not quantified as a delta here (its mixed result
does not provide a clean single baseline effect size from the material available in this session).

## Positioning
The advance is empirical-confirmatory + design: (a) a robust, alpha-controlled positive phase 3 for
donanemab, (b) validation of tau-PET stratification (low/medium vs high tau) as an enrollment and
analysis axis, and (c) a limited-duration dosing regimen (stop on amyloid clearance).
