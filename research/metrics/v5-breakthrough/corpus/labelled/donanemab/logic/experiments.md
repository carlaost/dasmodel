# Experiments / measured-outcomes ledger

Primary empirical study: TRAILBLAZER-ALZ 2, a phase 3 RCT. NO figure/table digitization was
possible (full text is paywalled); all numbers below are transcribed verbatim from the JAMA
structured abstract (evidence/abstract.txt). Q-IDs -> gro/quantities.yaml.

## Design
- Multicenter: 277 medical research centers/hospitals in 8 countries.
- Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, 18-month phase 3.
- Enrolled n=1736 with early symptomatic AD (MCI/mild dementia), amyloid+ and low/medium or high
  tau on PET; enrollment June 2020 - November 2021; last primary-outcome visit April 2023.
- 1:1 randomization: donanemab n=860, placebo n=876; IV every 4 weeks for 72 weeks; donanemab
  switched to placebo (blinded) upon meeting dose-completion (amyloid-clearance) criteria.
- Primary outcome: change in iADRS (range 0-144; lower = worse) baseline -> 76 weeks.
- Key secondary: change in CDR-SB (range 0-18; higher = worse).
- 24 gated outcomes; alpha .04 to low/medium-tau population outcomes, .01 to combined-population outcomes.

## Efficacy results (LSM change at 76 weeks)
E1 iADRS, low/medium tau: donanemab -6.02 (95% CI -7.01 to -5.03); placebo -9.27 (95% CI -10.23 to
   -8.31); difference 3.25 (95% CI 1.88-4.62); P < .001.   [Q01,Q02,Q03]
E2 iADRS, combined: donanemab -10.2 (95% CI -11.22 to -9.16); placebo -13.1 (95% CI -14.10 to
   -12.13); difference 2.92 (95% CI 1.51-4.33); P < .001.  [Q04,Q05,Q06]
E3 CDR-SB, low/medium tau: donanemab 1.20 (95% CI 1.00-1.41); placebo 1.88 (95% CI 1.68-2.08);
   difference -0.67 (95% CI -0.95 to -0.40); P < .001.     [Q07,Q08,Q09]
E4 CDR-SB, combined: donanemab 1.72 (95% CI 1.53-1.91); placebo 2.42 (95% CI 2.24-2.60);
   difference -0.7 (95% CI -0.95 to -0.45); P < .001.      [Q10,Q11,Q12]
E5 Gated-outcome hierarchy: 23 of 24 gated outcomes statistically significant. [Q13,Q14]

## Safety results
S1 ARIA-E (edema/effusion): donanemab 205 (24.0%; 52 symptomatic); placebo 18 (2.1%; 0 symptomatic
   during study).                                          [Q15,Q16,Q17]
S2 Infusion-related reactions: donanemab 74 (8.7%); placebo 4 (0.5%).  [Q18,Q19]
S3 Treatment-related deaths: donanemab 3; placebo 1.       [Q20,Q21]

## Population / retention
P1 n=1736 randomized; mean age 73.0 years; 996 (57.4%) women; 1182 (68.1%) low/medium tau; 552
   (31.8%) high tau; 1320 (76%) completed.                 [Q22-Q27]
